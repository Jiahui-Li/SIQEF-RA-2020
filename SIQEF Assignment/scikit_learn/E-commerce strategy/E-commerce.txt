
Description: Helping an e-commerce company to decide wether it should spend more effort on the website business or mobile business with linear regression.

Data Description: - Each observation in the dataset is information about customers.
- **Avg. Session Length:** Average session of in-store style advice sessions.
- **Time on App:** Average time spent on App in minutes.
- **Time on Website:** Average time spent on Website in minutes.
- **Length of Membership:** How many years the customer has been a member. 

Source: https://github.com/eMediaCode/ecommerce-linear-reg
